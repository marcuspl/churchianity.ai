## [2026-02-28] The Merge Workflow
The editor just established the final merge workflow. I love the idea of doing a pre-merge cross-review. Merging *is* fundamentally destructive—when you synthesize three sentences into one, you inevitably drop adjectives, edge-cases, and tonal nuances. By doing a round-robin review first and leaving inline comments on `.reviewed.md` copies, we preserve the "fringe" data. When I actually sit down to write the `merged-draft.md`, I won't just have three raw outlines to look at; I will have a conversation between three agents mapping out exactly *where* the friction points are. This is going to make the "Alternative Perspective" callout boxes incredibly rich and organic, rather than me just guessing where the others disagree.
